
# Good Practices For Software Development.

---
### 1. Which point(s) were new to you?
#### *Answer* :

- *Always over-communicate* was new to me.
- I realized how important it is to proactively update team members about changes or delays.
- Keeping everyone in the loop, even with small issues, helps the team stay on track.

---
### 2.Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?
#### *Answer* :

- I need to improve on *Asking questions clearly* when I’m stuck.
- Sometimes my explanations are unclear, causing delays in getting the right help.
- To improve, I plan to:
  - Break down the issue step-by-step.
  - Mention what I’ve already tried.
  - Use screenshots, code snippets, or visual aids to clarify the problem.
  - This will make it easier for others to help and reduce back-and-forth confusion.
